ReEntryApp Catalyst:

The Honorable Judge Ann Aiken, Chief Judge of the Federal District Court of Oregon

Judge Aiken launched the District of Oregon's Reentry Court, a pilot program for drug offenders, in partnership with U.S. Probation, the U.S. Attorney's office, the Federal Defender's office, private and non-profit community organizations, and others.

Judge Aiken called for the creation of a mobile app for to support and enhance participantion in the Reentry Court program. Judge Aiken posed that innovation challenge to the [Data Privacy Legal Hackathon] held in Brooklyn, NY the weekend of February 8th, 2014. Several individual participants at the event answered the call and formed a team together to develop a conceptual design and architecture for the app. When all projects were presented and judged at the conclusion of the event, the team's design and proposed approach won second place.

And so it was that the ReEntryApp project was formed.
